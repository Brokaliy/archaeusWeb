The story

About Archaeus

A Linux distribution built on the belief that power and simplicity are not opposites.

Why it exists

The problem with Linux

Arch Linux is, by many measures, the best Linux distribution available. Rolling releases, a minimal base, the AUR, excellent documentation. The catch: getting it to a usable desktop requires hours of reading wikis and editing config files before you can do anything.

Most "user-friendly" distributions solve this by adding complexity on top — heavy desktop environments, app stores that abstract too much, update managers that break things. Archaeus takes a different approach.

Keep the good parts of Arch. Add a control center that makes the common tasks visual. Touch nothing else.

"The terminal isn't going anywhere. We're just making it optional."
— 7n7, creator of Archaeus
Add a GUI, remove nothing

Every feature of base Arch is intact. pacman, yay, the AUR, manual config files — all there.

Transparent actions

Every click in the ACC runs a real shell command. Nothing is hidden — you can see exactly what it did.

Opinionated defaults

Archaeus ships with choices already made — Hyprland, Kitty, Inter font, the warm palette. Good choices, out of the box.

Beautiful as a priority

The aesthetic isn't an afterthought. The warm dark palette, circular window controls, and clean fonts are part of the spec.

Open source, always

Archaeus is fully open source. The ISO build scripts, ACC source, and all configs are on GitHub.

Timeline

How we got here

2025 — The frustration
Arch is great. Getting there isn't.

After spending hours setting up Arch for the third time — manually partitioning, configuring Hyprland, hunting dotfiles — the question: why is there no distro that ships this, configured, with a GUI wrapper for the maintenance?

Late 2025 — The spec
Designing the Archaeus Control Center

The ACC concept takes shape: an Electron app with five panels covering everything a new user needs to configure — without touching config files. The aesthetic direction is set: warm dark, coffee shop, minimal.

Early 2026 — Build begins
archiso profile + ACC development

The ISO build pipeline, Hyprland config, Kitty theme, SDDM skin, and ACC Electron app are all in active development. First ISO builds are being tested in VMs.

Upcoming — v0.1.0
First public ISO release

The initial public release. A complete, bootable ISO with Hyprland, the ACC, and the full Archaeus aesthetic. Install on real hardware.
